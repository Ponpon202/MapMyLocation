Hermes (a.k.a Map My Location)

Hermes is an attempt to provide a sense of security to all the travelers of all over the world and especially the women in India who use the public transport to commute to their work place and back home. "Hermes" is an olympian god in Greek mythology an dis protector and patron of travelers. The application lets a commuter be tracked while on the road, posting the whereabouts at regular intervals on popular social network. The posts are secured and are visible to only those whom the commuter has provided permissions to view.
